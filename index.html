How to read this

Percentile compares each country with its own past, not with other countries. A value of 99 pct means that country is higher than about 99% of its own monthly history. Raw country GPR shows the original _all values, and the global line uses GPR_AI. Data source: Iacoviello and Tong AI-GPR downloads.
