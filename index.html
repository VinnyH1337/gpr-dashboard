How to read this

Percentile compares each country with its own past, not with other countries. A value of 99 pct means that country is higher than about 99% of its own monthly history. Raw country GPR stays visible as context, and the global line uses the monthly global GPR series. Data source: Iacoviello and Tong GPR downloads.
